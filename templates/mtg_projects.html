Budget Calculator:

This program scrubs TCGPlayer to find the cheapest printing available for each card in a deck.
It uses these prices to determine the "cheapest printing available" budget for your decklist


Talion Calculator:

This program scrubs edhREC for the most played cards to analyze what number is best to select when playing the card Talion, the Kindly Lord


Deck Comparisons:

This program takes any numbner of commander decklist URLs, and analyzes whether or not they will form a balanced pod when playing against one another.
If not, it will make suggestions on what changes can be made to form a more balanced pod


Deck Uniqueness:

This program takes a commander decklist URL, and compares the list to the edhREC page for that commander.
It shows how many cards are unique to the average decklist for the deck, as well as how many cards are unique to the overall edhREC page
